Welcome to my profile!

Hi! My name is Arthur, I am 18 years old, I come from Monaco, and I'm a first year student at ESSEC Business School in the BBA program. I'm passionate about cinema, and art. I also love travelling and discovering new cultures. I'm also learning to code and I hope to get better and better!

Cinema

Since I was a kid, I have always enjoyed going to the cinema. It is one of my favourite activities to do. The recent movies I saw include Jojo Rabitt, by Taika Waititi, Trey Edward Shults' Waves, Bong Joon Ho's award-winning Parasite, 1917 by Sam Mendes, and The Farewell, directed by Lulu Wang. My favourite film so far has been Parasite, because of the extremely well done editing, the storyline, the ryhthm of the movie and the performance of the actors.

Art

One of my favourite hobbies is going to museums and spending hours inside, especially in Le Louvre. My favourite artist (at the moment) is David Hockney.

Travelling

I have been to places like New York City, Washington DC, Barbados, Norway, and other countries in Europe with my family. I especially enjoy discovering new cultures, food, and exploring the architecture in other cities of the world. I also particularly enjoy going to the museums when I'm travelling because I get to see art that is not in the museums I usually go to. My favourite place I've been to is New York City, by far, and I hope to live in it later in life.

Charitable work experiences

One of the things I most enjoy is giving back and helping others. Encouraged by my teachers and family, during more than 5 years, I participated in the creation of a musical show during which the profits were given to help malnourished and poor kids in Madagascar get a proper education and have safe environment to grow up in. Moreover, participating in PeaceJam's One Billion Acts of Peace allowed me to meet with laureates of the Nobel Peace Prize and to collect funds to help girls in Malawi.
